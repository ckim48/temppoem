Acrostic Rules

How to write: Create an acrostic poem by choosing a word or phrase, writing it vertically, and using each letter to begin a line. Each line should relate to respective letter, forming a meaningful composition.

Example: Title “Joyful Moments” (Theme: SHARE)

  • Smiles all around,
  • Happiness we have found.
  • A joyful heart,
  • Ring laughter like art.
  • Easy moments, pure and smart.
